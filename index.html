About Me

My name is Mark R. Luoma. I am a born-and-raised native from Geneva, OH and I'm crazy about Cleveland. My journey that's led me to where I am today has been an adventurous one and I'm excited to step foot into the web development world. As a college graduate from the University of Mount Union, my biology degree has led me to various worksites in various cities such as Tampa, FL and Gulf Shores, AL. Aside from my education and work history, I am a nature enthusiast who enjoys all things outside. During the (short) warmer months in Ohio you can find me on a golf course, fishing on the lake, or hiking in the woods. During the (long) winter months I can be found snowboarding in NY or binge watching 'The Office' for the 100th time. I'm a down-to-earth guy who enjoys nothing more than a day outside topped with a craft brew and a few Barrio tacos.

What I Am

Developer

It took me 28 years to jump into web development and I'm glad it didn't take me 29. Becoming a web developer from scratch has been nothing short of amazing. I enjoy the everyday challenges the field brings and being able to show an end protect brings me great joy. I take great pride in managing time, executing deadlines, and working with my teams to bring a final product to the table. With the new rise of the sun, I look forward to learning something new in the ever-changing technology world.

Teacher

Empowering others through teaching is one of the greatest thing's this life has to offer. As a teacher , working with children grades 6-12, I've been able to take ideas and methods and express them in a simple and understandable manner. Teaching molded me to be a better listener, educator, and overall person; and when a student's light bulb would go off and idea's began to click, that is what truly made me enjoy the art of teaching.

Biologist

Science is my guilty pleasure. With a Bachelors of Science degree from the University of Mount Union, biology and chemisty were always my first love. Just like the technology scene, science is always changing and revolutionizing the world. I have skills ranging from laboratory methods, animal husbandry, microbiology, and even enviornmental testing. Becoming a biologist is one of my greatest accomplishments and I'm excited to blend my skills gained into the technology scene.
